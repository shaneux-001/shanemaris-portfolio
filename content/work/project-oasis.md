Project Oasis is Southwest's initiative to bring a new premium brand experience to its lounge product. As Digital Product Manager, I led the team responsible for standing up the new theme — a ground-up design and technology effort to establish a distinct premium visual language within the Southwest ecosystem.

## The challenge

Building a premium experience within an established brand requires careful balance. The product needs to feel differentiated — elevated, distinct, suited to a premium context — while remaining recognizably Southwest. That tension shapes every decision in the design system: color, typography, component behavior, and the overall aesthetic register of the experience.

## My role

As DPM, I was responsible for the product direction of the initiative — defining scope, aligning stakeholders, managing the team's roadmap, and connecting the design decisions to the business goals of the premium program. I worked closely with design and engineering leads to ensure the theme architecture was sound and that the team had clarity on priorities.

## Status

Project Oasis is ongoing and has not been publicly announced. Details available in conversation.
